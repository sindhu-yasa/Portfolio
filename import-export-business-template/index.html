Application Purpose

Designed for practical import-export workflows

This template supports the business path from first inquiry to shipment planning. Customers can explore services, calculate estimated landed cost, submit cargo details, review document requirements, and track a sample shipment.

Lead Capture

Quote and support forms collect structured customer details that can be connected to a CRM or email workflow.

Trade Operations

Shipment intake fields cover cargo type, Incoterms, transport mode, packaging, dates, and special handling.

Customer Self-Service

Customers can check documentation, understand Incoterms, estimate costs, and follow shipment milestones.

Operating Model

From inquiry to delivery

  1. AssessCustomer submits origin, destination, commodity, volume, and timing.
  2. QuoteOperations estimates freight, insurance, duties, taxes, and handling.
  3. PrepareDocuments are checked against shipment type and destination requirements.
  4. MoveShipment milestones are shared with the customer from pickup to delivery.
